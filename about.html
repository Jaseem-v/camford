Who we are

The Camford Institute of Management is an ISO 9001:2008 certified institute and FIVE STAR rating Pradhan Manthri Koushal Vikas Yojana - PMKVY, Govt. of India accredited training centre. CIM is a fast growing educational organisation specializing in the fields of Management Education, Corporate Training, Advanced Computer Education, English Language Training, Soft Skills Development programmes and Human Resource Management programmes with a strong focus on the present and future requirements of the job market.

Our training programs enables our students to acquire world class employability skills, business management and administrative qualification, advanced IT skills, entrepreneurship skills and business communication that would enhance careers of students in the management and IT sector, the unique education methodology and the compact, integrated educational programmes shall be a great advantage to all those who look forward to achieve early success in their life.

We offer a number of training programmes with well structured curriculums that foster effective learning and focus ultimately on opening the doors to success and a bright career ahead.

All of our Advanced Computer Training programs are accredited by the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is a research and development organization under the Department of Electronics and Information Technology, Govt. of India.

All of our Management Education programmes are accredited by the Bharath Sevak Samaj - BSS is the National Development Agency sponsored by the Planning Commission, Government of India to ensure public co-operation for implementing government plans. The constitution and functioaning of Bharat Sevak Samaj is approved unanimously by the Indian Parliament.

Camford Institute of Management's pre-placement training and corporate grooming program provide emphasis on the emotional and psychology aspects of the students so that they are able to present themselves optimally, at the interview board.

The institute is actively involved in conducting seminars, events, training and development programmes for reputed corporate and educational institutes. Students are actively involved in these events to acquaint themselves with the latest industry trends and skills that will benefit them once placed.
