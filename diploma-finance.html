PG Diploma in Financial Management

Course Details

The course prepares you for relatively new finance-associated jobs that are finding their way in the new digitized world.

The program takes you through the key concepts of financial management and gives you enough coverage to perform at all levels of book keeping, financing, cash flow, budgeting, accounting, reporting etc. The curriculum of this course is designed by team of experienced finance and academic professionals from various domains.
